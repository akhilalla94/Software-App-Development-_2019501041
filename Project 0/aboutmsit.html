Master of Science in Information Technology is a two year post graduate program offered by a Consortium of Institutions of Higher Learning. The program prepares students, of all branches of engineering, to industry ready technologists. Under MoU with CMU, some Carnegie Mellon University researchers are providing guidance on course content.

Following are the list of Courses for the first year in MSIT.

Courses

  2. Computer Science Principles and Programming - 1
  3. Introduction to Data Science
  4. JAVA(OOPS)
  5. Algorithms and Data Structure-1
  6. DataBases
  7. Software Engineering Foundation
  8. Algorithms and Data Structure-2
  9. Introduction to Operating System
  10. Computer Network Foundation
  11. Introduction to Cyber Security
  12. Software App Development
